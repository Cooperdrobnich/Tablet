UX Internship

Overview

*Due to NDA I cannot share everything regarding the work and projects I was a part of. Please contact me for further details.*


Throughout the summer of 2021, I worked at Credibly as UX research intern. Credibly is a finance-tech lending company that leverages cutting-edge data science, technology, partner relations, and customer support to provide business owners with accelerated access to right-sized capital solutions.

Outcome

During my time at Credibly, I was exposed to many different in-house projects and as a result, I was able to gain valuable training in optimizing the customer's experience. Due to NDA, I currently cannot share all the specifics here, but these are some impacts that I made within the marketing team throughout my internship:


  • Conducted target page analysis, utilizing click maps to determine where users are attempting to navigate, and boosted user flow accordingly.
  • Conducted user navigation analysis, utilizing click maps to determine where and how users are actually getting to their desired destination.
  • Worked with user navigation and target page insights to create illustrations proposing new call to action buttons and links, while also revising current placements.
  • Created and analyzed user scroll maps across different product pages, creating benchmarks for where specific information would be best located from page to page.
  • Worked with google analytics to uncover AVG. time on page, visit percents, and bounce rates by device type.
  • Created navigation benchmarks by device type and reviewed new additions within each.
